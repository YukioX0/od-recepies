Lasagna Recipe

This is a detailed recipe for lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef and onion until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the beef mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
