Vadapao Recipe

This is a detailed recipe for vadapao.

Ingredients:

  • Pav buns
  • Spiced potato filling
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat the pav buns on a griddle with butter until golden brown.
  2. Prepare the spiced potato filling by mashing boiled potatoes and mixing with spices.
  3. Slice the pav buns horizontally.
  4. Spread green chutney on one side and tamarind chutney on the other side of the bun.
  5. Place a generous amount of the spiced potato filling between the buns.
  6. Serve hot with extra chutneys on the side.
